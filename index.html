2023

VacuLift Robot

At a competitive robot battle for high school students, I participated as the designer and assembler of my team's robot. The competition required robots to perform tasks such as line-following, lifting, and placing objects into designated positions, demanding both precision and speed.

The highlight of my design was an innovative idea to use a vacuum mechanism instead of the traditional servo gripper. This approach not only significantly reduced operation time but also enhanced accuracy in object manipulation. Thanks to this groundbreaking design and its superior efficiency, my team successfully secured victory in the competition.

Role

Designer and assembler of components

2023

A Toolkit Use For Laboratory

During my high school years, I worked on a project involving embedded systems with the idea of collecting data from physics and chemistry experiments through sensors, then synthesizing and displaying it on graphs. The goal was to make learning natural science subjects more convenient and intuitive for students.

My project was highly praised by professors, and I had the opportunity to present it at the National Science and Engineering Fair. I was then recognized for this research by the Ministry of Education and Training of Vietnam.

Hi there!

Im Duc, a passionate Energy Technologist with a clear vision for the future: advancing the field of Nuclear Engineering. My primary research focuses on nuclear fuel management and radioactive waste disposaltwo critical areas shaping the sustainability of global energy systems.

Driven by a love for discovery and problem-solving, I continuously explore innovative solutions to tackle complex energy challenges. While my academic and professional foundation lies in Energy Technology, my curiosity extends into Embedded Systems Hardware, where I experiment with building simple robots and devices to streamline everyday tasks. These projects fuel my creative side and help me approach engineering problems with a fresh perspective.

Im known for my methodical and professional approach, always blending meticulous planning with a touch of creativity to ensure exceptional results. I also prioritize staying updated with industry trends, as I believe lifelong learning is essential in todays fast-paced technological world.

But life isnt all about work for me. At home, I find peace in maintaining a clean, organized spaceits my way of creating clarity and focus. On the other hand, I dream of exploring the world, with Barcelona, Spain, at the top of my travel list. Im captivated by the citys architectural wonders, especially the Sagrada Famlia, and cant wait to experience the electrifying atmosphere of Camp Nou as I cheer for my lifelong favorite team, FC Barcelona.

Through my journey, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the field of nuclear energy while continuing to discover and create along the way. Lets connect and explore how we can innovate together!
