Mini Dinosaur Game

In my first year of university, I stumbled upon the idea of creating a game simulating Chrome's dinosaur game. I developed a game titled "Mini Dinosaur Game."

In the program, I used the Mediapipe library to implement hand tracking, applying it to computer vision. Additionally, I utilized PyQt5 to design the game interface.

Oct 2024

Vietnamese Time Usage

To visualize "What a Vietnamese person does in a day," I wrote a program using the Python programming language to create a visualization of raw data obtained from The World Bank.

In the program, I represented the key activities performed by 1,500 surveyed individuals in a day using moving bubbles. I used the Pandas library to read data from a CSV statistical file and the D3Blocks library to visualize the extracted data.

About me

Hi there! 👋

I’m Duc, a passionate Energy Technologist with a clear vision for the future: advancing the field of Nuclear Engineering. My primary research focuses on nuclear fuel management and radioactive waste disposal—two critical areas shaping the sustainability of global energy systems.

Driven by a love for discovery and problem-solving, I continuously explore innovative solutions to tackle complex energy challenges. While my academic and professional foundation lies in Energy Technology, my curiosity extends into Embedded Systems Hardware, where I experiment with building simple robots and devices to streamline everyday tasks. These projects fuel my creative side and help me approach engineering problems with a fresh perspective.

I’m known for my methodical and professional approach, always blending meticulous planning with a touch of creativity to ensure exceptional results. I also prioritize staying updated with industry trends, as I believe lifelong learning is essential in today’s fast-paced technological world.

But life isn’t all about work for me. At home, I find peace in maintaining a clean, organized space—it’s my way of creating clarity and focus. On the other hand, I dream of exploring the world, with Spain, at the top of my travel list. I’m captivated by the city’s architectural wonders, especially the Sagrada Família, and can’t wait to experience the electrifying atmosphere of Camp Nou as I cheer for my lifelong favorite team, FC Barcelona.

Through my journey, I aim to contribute meaningfully to the field of energy while continuing to discover and create along the way. Let’s connect and explore how we can innovate together!
